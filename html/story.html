Our Story

Where it began?

In a small town in Sweden, the two brothers Max and Tim Svensson grew upp never finding cooking a fun activity in the same way they found eating of said cooking. They wanted to change this and so in 2012 they decided to found a company specializing in this area. Making it fun to cook.

Who are we?

Smaksak is a Swedish food subscription delivery service of now over 500 employees all over Scandinavia. A company valuing the simplicity, honesty and passion for foodmaking and for enjoying the small moments that come with it. We are a diverse mixture of people who share the passion for food and the joy it can bring.

What we believe in?

We believe in a sustainable world where we take our responsibility by shopping and eating sustaianbly produced groceries fresh off the local markets. We believe in the power of togetherness, of learning and earning and what music can do to a setting.

What we strive for

The main goal of Smaksak is being the go-to delivery service for homecooked meals in Scandinavia. We strive to be a trustworthy and of high quality service for all of our customers. We strive to be a supporter for every person on our planet no matter where you are. We strive to wake the passion within every single one of you.

About Us

Smaksak is a food subscription delivery service based in Jönköping, Sweden. Founded by the Svensson brothers Max and Tim in 2012, it has become the go-to food delivery service companies in Scandinavia. Enjoy the small moments with us.
